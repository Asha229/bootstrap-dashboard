Fitness Discounts, benefit from being active

My Gym Discounts is a voluntary, self-paid gym membership scheme. You can benefit from corporate rates and you pay the club directly like a normal membership.

It offers discounts at over 3,500 gyms across the UK and Ireland with different membership schemes available Participating gyms include PureGym, Fitness First, Nuffield Health, Virgin Active and Living Well health clubs.

  • Discounted Fitbit
  • Clothing and Footwear
  • Discounted online fitness workouts
  • Fitness equipment for your home
  • Discounted nutritional supplements and supplies eg HelloFresh, Musclefood, TasteCard, Gusto, Graze

To access corporate rates in the local area, you simply need to log on to My Gym Discounts (use the company reference BHSF) and select your chosen gym and download the discount voucher. The chosen Gym will contact you direct to arrange you to visit the gym, look around and if you like it, you can join using your voucher to access the special rates.
